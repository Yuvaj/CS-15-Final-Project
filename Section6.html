Section 6: Final Documentation & Reflection

Yuvraj Singh – Robotic Arm

Final Documentation:

The project documentation compiles all key stages: from proposal to testing. It includes the Arduino and Processing code, system design, component list, and evaluation strategy. All pages on the website provide detailed insights into the development process.

Reflection:

This project taught me about both hardware and software integration. I faced real engineering issues such as power optimization and signal processing. Even though I couldn’t complete the 3D-printed housing, the electronic system proved functional. I learned the importance of structured coding, clear documentation, and iterative testing, I’m proud of the skills and problem-solving that I have demonstrated but unsatisfied with the missing 3D printing aspect of my project.
