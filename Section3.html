Section 3: Design and Analysis

Yuvraj Singh – Robotic Arm

3.1 Decomposition of the Project:

  • Input: Angles sent via Processing sliders over serial communication
  • Processing: Arduino parses angle data, maps values, and transmits PWM signals to each servo via PCA9685
  • Output: Physical movement of servos to desired angles

3.2 Website Design:

Pages include Home, Section1–6, Code, About, and Procedure. Each page is styled with consistent CSS and structured with headings, divs, and navigational elements. Wireframes were planned for usability and readability.

3.3 Integration of Skills:

This section ties together structured programming through Arduino and client-side scripting through HTML/CSS, showcasing hardware and software skills. The design aligns with real-world engineering and coding.
